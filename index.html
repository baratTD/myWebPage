About Me

Hello, my name is Barat Ali. Here is a glimpse into my personal and professional journey so far.

I was born in an isolated village in Afghanistan during the civil war, with no access to hospitals. My early life was marked by significant challenges, including a battle with polio. Despite these obstacles, I refused to let them define me. Instead, I turned them into sources of strength.

At the age of 16, I arrived in Australia as a refugee. This was a turning point in my life, opening the door to new opportunities. I embraced this chance and pursued my education, eventually earning a Bachelor of Animation from Griffith University.

Breaking into the film industry as a Rigger was more than just a career choice for me. From the war-torn landscapes of my homeland to the creative spaces of renowned companies like Animal Logic, my journey has been one of resilience and passion.

With over seven years of experience, I specialize in character rigging, including biped, quadruped, bird, reptile, creature, and mechanical rigging for films, commercials, and video games. I am currently working at Animal Logic in Sydney, Australia.
