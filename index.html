About Me

In the midst of Afghanistan's civil war, my life unfolded. I was born in an isolated village without access to hospitals, and my existence was immediately marked by challenges, further compounded by the presence of polio. Rather than allowing these obstacles to define my identity, I transformed them into wellsprings of strength.

At the age of 16, I arrived in Australia as an asylum seeker, turning adversity into stepping stones for an educational journey that ultimately culminated in a Bachelor of Animation from Griffith University.

My foray into the film industry as a Rigger was more than just a profession; it served as a canvas for the fusion of determination. From the desolation of war-torn landscapes to the cinematic wonders crafted by renowned companies like Animal Logic, my narrative unfolded in frames of resilience.

Professional Life

I am a dedicated rigging artist with over seven years of experience, specializing in character rigging for films, commercials, and video games. My expertise spans biped, quadruped, bird, reptile, creature, and mechanical rigging. Currently, I am proud to be part of the team at Animal Logic in Sydney, Australia.

Co-founder of Global Refugee Academy

Beyond my professional achievements, I am deeply committed to supporting refugees. This dedication led to co-founding the Global Refugee Academy, a non-profit organization aimed at breaking educational barriers for displaced communities worldwide. Through this initiative, I advocate for refugees, helping them navigate their new lives and offering them opportunities for growth and integration.

The Global Refugee Academy provides education, resources, and support to refugees, helping them integrate into new communities and rebuild their lives. Our programs include vocational training, language courses, and cultural integration workshops. As a co-founder, I am involved in strategic planning, fundraising, and program development. My work includes coordinating with volunteers, partnering with local organizations, and overseeing project implementation.

One of our success stories involves a young refugee who, through our vocational training program, secured a job in the local community and is now an active member of society. These stories inspire me to continue our work and strive for greater impact.

If you are interested in supporting our cause, please visit our website or follow us on social media. Together, we can make a difference.
